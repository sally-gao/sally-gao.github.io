What can data science tell us about the legal conversation surrounding data privacy?

To investigate how data privacy is being addressed in the legal sphere, I used using a hierarchical clustering algorithm called HDBSCAN to cluster over two thousand Practical Law documents published between 2013 and 2018.

This analysis was included in a 2018 World Economic Forum publication called Our Shared Digital Future.

Analyzing recipe ingredients with Latent Dirichlet Allocation

To discover frequently-occurring ingredient combinations among thousands of recipes, I developed a way to came up with a way to represent recipes as "bag of words"-style documents and used a generative topic modeling technique called Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA).

Recipes were scraped using Python; data cleaning, textual parsing, and modeling done in R.
